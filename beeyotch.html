Wattpad is a social reading platform that allows users to read and publish their own tales online. The platform, which was founded by Allen Lau and Ivan Yuen, intends to build social groups around stories and break down barriers between readers and writers. People can compose and publish stories on the platform, or simply read stories created by other users. I've been a wattpad reader for almost 5 years. As I explore Wattpad, I've come across some deserving authors who I'd want to promote to you. All of their experiences are relatable and worthwhile to read. Some of the authors have millions of followers, while others have only a few hundred. Nonetheless, this does not define the values of their stories.

CONTROL THE GAME

Charisse Faith Viste believes in working hard. She does not believe in luck, only hard work. Bata pa lang siya, nasanay na siya na pinaghihirapan lahat ng meron siya. Being the only girl in a family full of guys, nasanay na siya na kung gusto niyang makuha, kailangan niyang pagtrabahuhan. And because of this, she grew up tough. She already accepted the fact that guys are just too intimidated to even give her a second glance. She'll always be too smart, too confident, too much for everyone. One day, she had this overwhelming feeling of sadness. She looked around and saw that everyone around her has someone by their side.... Everyone has someone to embrace... Siya? Libro lang ang kayakap. She used to be fine with the idea of being alone. alone, not lonely. But not anymore. She needed someone to stop her from feeling so lonely.
