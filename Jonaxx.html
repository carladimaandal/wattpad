Wattpad is a social reading platform that allows users to read and publish their own tales online. The platform, which was founded by Allen Lau and Ivan Yuen, intends to build social groups around stories and break down barriers between readers and writers. People can compose and publish stories on the platform, or simply read stories created by other users. I've been a wattpad reader for almost 5 years. As I explore Wattpad, I've come across some deserving authors who I'd want to promote to you. All of their experiences are relatable and worthwhile to read. Some of the authors have millions of followers, while others have only a few hundred. Nonetheless, this does not define the values of their stories.

Why do you Hate me?

If you hate something, would you change it? And if you change it, will you like it? Hindi alam ni Charity kung bakit ayaw na ayaw sa kanya ni Jayden Corpuz. Hindi pa kailanman ito nangyari sa buhay niya. Simula pagkabata, mahal na siya ng mga taong nakapaligid sa kanya. Ni isa, mapa babae o lalaki, wala siyang naging hater. At ngayong 21 years old na siya, saka pa siya magkakaroon ng hater? At sa katauhan pa ng lalaking gusto niya? How did that happen? Lahat ng gusto niya ay nakukuha niya, pero bakit ang isang ito, nairita sa kahit simpleng paghawi niya ne buhok?
